--- layout: scd title: 1.4.8.1.5.3 trackArtistRole permalink: /scd/trackartistrole scd-label: Track Artist Role scd-def: The role the artist plays on the track as listed on the CD packaging. scd-imp: Optional, Repeatable --- Comment The role may or might not be listed on the CD packaging. If the specific role isn’t listed in reference to a specific track DO NOT use the artist role(s) listed in 1.6.1.3 musicArtistRole. For example, an artist may be listed as an accordion player for the album overall but only specifically listed as a vocalist for the track, thus “accordion” should not be listed as a role under trackArtistRole. Value Control Use the terminology as listed on the CD such as “yodeling” or “tamburitza prim”
