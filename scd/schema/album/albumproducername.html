--- layout: scd title: 1.4.5.1 albumProducerName permalink: /scd/albumproducername scd-label: Album Producer Name scd-def: The name of the person, company, or service that produced the album. scd-imp: Mandatory --- Comment If the producer can be inferred or is stated to be the same as the solo artist or music group (i.e. self-published), use the name associated with the group/solo artist. Value Control As most entities involved in these CDs cannot be found in value authorities, record the name as displayed on the CD packaging. Personal names should be in their inverted form. Example Switchback Records; Kanbergs, Karlis; MP3.com
