I graduated with a Master's in Library and Information Science from Kent State University in May 2020. My focus was on digital libraries, archives and museums, particularly the development and support of digital initiatives. These include everything from applying user needs analyses to the creation and redesign of institutions' websites to developing and implementing collection metadata.

Projects

Explore the variety of projects that I worked on during my MLIS such as the creation of metadata schemas, a cultural heritage website, and user needs analyses.

Practicum

This culminating graduate course provides a supervised opportunity to apply theory, technical and practical knowledge in the professional field. My focus was in metadata and digital initiatives within an academic library's special collections.
