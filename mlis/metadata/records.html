scd001: "Alligator Necklace" - In Harms Way

description: "Alligator Necklace" is an album by In Harms Way. The CD was purchased in Warren, Ohio at the Menagerie Thrift & Gift Shopee. It was created through MP3.com's now defunct CD production service in 2000. It is a great example of the early 2000's online music environment.

scd002: "Everyone's Choice IV" - Mahoning Valley Button Box Club

description: "Everyone's Choice - Volume IV" is an album by the Mahoning Valley Button Box Club that was produced by Peppermint Records and released in 2006. Both the band and this particular album are National Cleveland Style Polka Hall of Fame inductees.

scd003: "Whips of Karma" - Karl Kanbergs

description: "Whips of Karma" is a self-published solo album released in 2008 in Portland, Oregon. It features original lyrics and music by Karlis Kanbergs, primarily in the folk and folk rock genres. The CD was purchased in Warren, Ohio at the Menagerie Thrift Store. It features a printer paper insert and a photo of the artist with his guitars.

scd006: "New York City Boy" - Evel de Musica

description: "New York City Boy" is a self-published DJ mixset by "Evel de Musica" in Rome, Italy. The music consists of simple remixes of popular music.
